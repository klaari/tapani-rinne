Space and the sound of It has been one of the cornerstones of
the music of Tapani Rinne and Teho Majamäki since the beginning.
The two previous albums contain meditative music recorded in
acoustically interesting and atmospheric locations - temples and
caves in India, as well as deep inside the country in the
railway tunnels of the Helsinki metropolitan area.
